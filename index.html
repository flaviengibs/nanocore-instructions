NanoCore Instructions

Welcome to NanoCore! In this game, you are a microscopic nanomachine injected into a mysterious human body. Your mission is to collect nutrients, survive hostile viruses, and ultimately uncover the secret hidden deep in the DNA.

🎮 Controls

  • Arrow keys or WASD / ZQSD – Move your nanomachine
  • E – Scan the nearby area (reveals nearby nutrients)
  • Space – Move to the next zone (only after collecting all nutrients)

🧬 Objective

  • Explore the body one zone at a time.
  • Collect all nutrients in the current zone.
  • Once all nutrients are collected, press Space to proceed to the next zone.
  • Every few nutrients collected, you earn upgrade points to improve your nanomachine.
  • Survive until you reach the final challenge at the core of the DNA.

🧪 Nutrients

  • Green tiles.
  • Give you energy when collected.
  • Only visible after a scan or when very close.

🦠 Viruses

  • Red or purple enemies on the map.
  • Touching them drains your energy.
  • Some viruses move randomly and can change direction.

⚙️ Upgrades

  • Every 3 nutrients collected grants you 1 upgrade point.
  • Choose between:
    • +Speed – Move faster
    • +Max Energy – Increases your energy capacity
    • Passive Regen – Slowly regenerate energy over time
    • Extended Scan – Increases the scan radius

📡 Scanning

  • Press E to scan surrounding tiles.
  • Reveals hidden nutrients within a radius.
  • Has a cooldown between uses.

🚧 Zones

  • Each zone contains new nutrients and viruses.
  • You cannot move to the next zone until you’ve collected all nutrients in the current one.
  • Press Space to advance once all nutrients are collected.

🧠 Final Challenge

  • After several zones, you’ll reach the DNA core.
  • Collect 5 final special nutrients before your energy runs out.
  • If you succeed, you will decode the DNA and win the game!
